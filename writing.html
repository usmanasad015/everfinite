Nowadays mobile phone has become a necessity hence everybody has a mobile phone. Most people usually cover their phones with phone cases for phone protection. Doing so ensures the safety and a sense of satisfaction.

Clear phone cases do turn yellow and dirty with time, no matter how careful you are. If you are facing a similar issue, then you came to the right place.

Cleaning a clear phone case is not such a difficult task. You can easily clean it yourself in no time by following one of the following methods.

Clean a Clear Case Using Dish Soap and Water

  • Add two drops of dish soap to a bowl of hot water and stir it.
  • Scrub the outside of your case in a circular motion with a soft cloth or toothbrush. Scrub all the difficult-to-reach areas, such as the camera slot, and the button or charging port-holes.
  • Rinse and re-rinse as needed with running water to remove any soap. If scuffs and marks remain, apply more soapy water and then re-rinse it.
  • Once your case is clean, dry it with a soft cloth and allow it to air dry for 1 hour.

This method is quick to perform and recommended to try once a week for your case to make it look nice and clean.

Clean a Clear Case Using Baking Soda with Vinegar

  • Put your case on a clean towel.
  • Garnish baking soda onto the phone case.
  • Add 1/2 cup of vinegar and 1/2 cup of water. Let this mixture remain on the cover for 2 hours (for best results).
  • Then, using an old toothbrush, scrub the case.
  • Finally, wash the phone, dry it with a cloth, and let it air dry completely.

Clean a Clear Case Using Rubbing Alcohol

Rubbing or isopropyl alcohol is effective in removing stubborn stains that you cant remove with soapy water. At least, it will eliminate bacteria even if it doesn’t remove stains.

  • Scrub your phone case with small amounts of isopropyl alcohol on a soft cloth.
  • Another choice is to utilize a spray container to apply to rub alcohol straightforwardly to the case.
  • After you’ve finished cleaning the exterior and interior of the case, wipe it down with a soft dry cloth to remove any chemical left. The surface of your case should be dry.
  • Let it air dry for 1 hour before placing it back on your mobile phone.
  • In the end, allow your case to air dry in a well-ventilated area, or wipe it down with a dry microfiber cloth to soak up any leftover droplets.
